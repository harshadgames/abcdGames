Learn A-B-C-D

Click Or Tap to shoot

How to play

If you are playing the game on mobile, click the rocket when the balloon comes right above it. On a laptop, you can use the mouse click. The objective of the game is to pop the balloon containing the alphabet. When you hit the target alphabet successfully, you hear its pronunciation along with the name of the object that starts with the alphabet. This balloon shooting game for kids online falls under the category of simple click games, space back games, or simple touch games. Also, note that this is a perfect educational fun game to play on loop and keep kids engaged.
