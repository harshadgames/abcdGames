How to Play

In order to play the online ABC drawing game, touch the yellow dot with a mouse, touchpad, or touchscreen and move the touch point as per the guidelines presented. If you follow the directions correctly, you will successfully draw an alphabet. After each successful drawing of an alphabet, you will hear the sound of the alphabet and the name of an object starting with that letter.

"About the Game"

This is an online slate or drawing board for kids, which helps them learn to draw alphabets. As a parent, you do not need to dictate the steps to draw alphabets every time; kids are guided with clues, making it easy for them to follow this online ABC activity for preschoolers. This online phonics game has made teaching alphabets very easy for parents.
