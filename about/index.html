Who We Are

Welcome to ABCDgames! We are a passionate group of individuals dedicated to creating equal learning opportunities for all children, making education both efficient and fun. Our team is composed of professionals with over 13 years of corporate training experience, educators, and parents who are committed to enhancing the learning experiences of their kids. Most of our team works out of Pune, India



At ABCDgames, we believe in the power of playful learning. Our young and dynamic team is focused on making a positive impact on children's education by developing engaging and interactive games.

Our Vision and Mission

"Our Vision"

At ABCDgames, our mission is to create fun and educational games that are safe for kids and accessible to every child across the globe. We are committed to ensuring that all children have free access to learning opportunities. We understand that today's parents are highly aware of the importance of the content their children consume on the internet. They seek to leverage technology to make learning enjoyable and valuable for their kids. We aim to provide these parents with high-quality options that meet their needs.



Our development process places a heavy emphasis on understanding the unique learning styles of children across different age groups, from as young as 6 months to 5 years old. We design our games to be impactful, catering to the distinct ways in which children learn at each stage of their development.


To ensure our games are continually improving and meeting the needs of our young audience, we actively seek and value feedback from parents. This ongoing dialogue helps us refine our games and create the best possible learning experiences.

At ABCDgames, we are dedicated to making learning an exciting adventure that fosters growth, curiosity, and joy in every child.

"Vision"

We envision a world where kids across the globe have equal opportunities to learn essential skills such as language, mathematics, and soft skills. We are committed to harnessing technology to make learning efficient and impactful.


Our goal is to use technology to assess where each child may be falling behind and to create individualized learning paths tailored to their needs. At the same time, we aim to empower parents with the tools they need to understand and address gaps in their children's learning.


At ABCDgames, we are dedicated to ensuring that every child has the resources and support they need to succeed in their educational journey.
